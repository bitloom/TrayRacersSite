Toad and Seek Update

We’ve added a whole new game mode to celebrate World Environment Day as part of the Green Game Jam 2023.

The goal of the Green Game Jam 2023 is to protect 3 ecosystems and help safeguard the lives of harlequin toads, snow leopards, and manta rays.

This will be done in partnership with 8 local organisations that work in the Amazon, Himalayas, and the Western Indian Ocean.
